Farmers for Forests (F4F) is a hybrid social enterprise based in Pune, Maharashtra. Since 2019, we have been creating & supporting biodiverse farms & forests that significantly increase farmer income. We harness the power of farmers, forests and technology in our mission to fight climate-change and poverty, at scale!

Our work

Over 40% of India's workforce is engaged in agriculture. They are facing the brunt of climate-change. Erratic rainfall patterns, extreme weather events, degrading soils and diminishing water tables are all contributing to a significant decline in farmer yields and income.

We help farms bring back lost fertility and biodiversity on their farms, significantly increasing their climate-resilience. LEARN MORE
